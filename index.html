About the author

Lara Mac Veeameroi is a poet and author of short stories born in the late 80's. She has written since childhood, moving fluidly between three different languages – Italian, English and German. Her work explores a variety of themes, from her struggles with depression, the feeling of loneliness, the effort to find herself beneath layers of cultural conditioning and painful experiences, and her relentless pursuit of hope. An outdoors lover, she often draws imagery from the natural world to shape her poetry and stories. A keen supporter of nature and human rights, she identifies as sapphic.
